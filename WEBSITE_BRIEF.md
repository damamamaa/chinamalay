*   **Text (Dark):** `#1A1A1A`

### **Typography**
*   **Headings / Display:** *Playfair Display* (Serif, elegant, high contrast)
*   **Body Text:** *Inter* (Sans-serif, clean, modern, legible)
*   **Labels / Technical details:** *Courier New* or *Roboto Mono* (Monospace, adds a technical/precision feel)

### **Key Visual Elements**
*   **Split Screen Hero:** The homepage must immediately show the duality. One side nature/healing (TCM), one side cosmic/stars (Xin Jian).
*   **Dark Mode Default:** The site should feel immersive and cinematic, not like a white Word document.
*   **High-End Photography:** Use images of herbs, needles, ancient books, compasses (Luo Pan), and star maps.
*   **Noise Texture:** Add a subtle grain/noise overlay to all backgrounds to prevent them from looking "flat" or "digital".
